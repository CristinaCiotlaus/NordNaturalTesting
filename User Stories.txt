      As a new user, I want to register for an account so that I can access the website's features.
        As a registered user, I want to log in with my credentials to access my account.
        As a forgetful user, I want to reset my password if I forget it, so that I can regain access to my account.

        As a shopper, I want to update the quantity of products in my shopping cart, so that I can adjust my purchase quantities.
